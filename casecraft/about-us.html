CaseCraft was born out of frustration with cases that cracked, faded, and fell apart. We set out to make something better — and we haven't stopped since.

Who We Are

A Melbourne team that cares about every detail.

CaseCraft started in 2019 out of a small workshop in Fitzroy. Our founders — two product designers and a materials engineer — were tired of choosing between a case that looked good and one that actually protected their phones.

So they built both. Today we ship to customers across Australia and beyond, with every case designed, tested, and quality-checked in-house before it ever reaches your door.

Our Values

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2019

Founded in Fitzroy

Sarah, Marcus and Priya start prototyping cases from a shared workshop. First 100 units sold out in 48 hours via Instagram.

2020

First Online Store

CaseCraft.com.au goes live. Despite the pandemic, online orders surge as people look to personalise their WFH setups.

2022

Zero-Plastic Packaging

We eliminate all single-use plastics from our supply chain — two years ahead of our original target. Every box is now 100% compostable.

2024

50,000 Customers Milestone

We hit 50K orders and expand into New Zealand and Singapore. Our Leather Edition becomes our fastest-selling product ever.

2026

New Platform & What's Next

Brand new website, new collections, and an exciting product line launching later this year. Stay tuned.
